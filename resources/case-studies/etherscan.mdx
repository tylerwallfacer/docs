[Etherscan](https://etherscan.io/) is Ethereum's premier block explorer. Through the vaults.fyi API, Etherscan embeds contextual yield opportunity cards inside wallet address pages — surfacing what idle assets _could_ be doing, right when users are already examining their holdings.

## The Idea: Contextual Yield at the Point of Exploration

When users look up an Ethereum address, they're trying to understand what a wallet holds and how it's being used. That intent creates a natural opening to surface yield opportunities for assets sitting idle.

The Etherscan Cards integration uses vaults.fyi market data and idle-asset detection to show users relevant DeFi opportunities directly within the block explorer — without requiring them to visit a separate platform to discover yield.

## How It Works

1. **Detect idle balances.** When a user views a wallet address, the integration checks for idle ETH and stablecoin balances (USDC, USDT, DAI).
2. **Find relevant opportunities.** vaults.fyi identifies the top DeFi yield opportunities matching those assets, filtered by liquidity and maturity criteria.
3. **Display the card.** A Yield Opportunity card appears in the Cards tab of the wallet page — showing token type, protocol name, vault name, and 7-day APY. ([See an example on vitalik.eth](https://etherscan.io/address/0xd8dA6BF26964aF9D7eEd9e03E53415D37aA96045#cards).)
4. **Connect to vaults.fyi.** Users who click through land on vaults.fyi, where they can explore detailed analytics, deposit in one click, and track positions.

## Vault Selection Criteria

To ensure only high-signal opportunities are shown:

- TVL must exceed **\$100 million** — filtering out thin or unproven vaults.
- Stablecoin results are limited to USDC, USDT, and DAI underlyings.
- The vault with the highest 7-day APY for each asset type (ETH, stablecoins) is surfaced.

Simple, transparent criteria keep the experience trustworthy for Etherscan's broad user base.

## API Capabilities Used

- **Market data** — real-time APY, TVL, and vault metadata for vault discovery and display.
- **Portfolio and personalized insights** — idle balance detection and opportunity matching based on wallet holdings.
- **Transaction support** — enables users who click through to supply or withdraw directly on vaults.fyi.

## Why This Integration Works

Etherscan's users are already engaged with their wallet data. Surfacing yield opportunities at that moment — with vaults filtered for size and quality — adds value without disruption. Users don't need a separate product to discover DeFi yield; it appears in the context where they're already investigating their holdings.
