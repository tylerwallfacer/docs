## The Challenge: Proving Performance Claims in Onchain Finance

When building investment products, asset managers face a fundamental trust problem: how do you prove to potential investors that your performance figures are accurate and unbiased?

Traditional finance answers this with auditing firms and regulatory oversight. Onchain asset management needed a different approach.

As Cormac Daly, Founder of Nashpoint, explained:

> _"Using vaults.fyi data on our site is like saying we're audited by such and such... our APYs and data are independently verified by a third party."_

Without a neutral data provider, Nashpoint would have had to build and maintain its own analytics stack — and investors would still have had reason to question the numbers.

## The Solution: Independent Validation via vaults.fyi

Nashpoint uses the vaults.fyi API to power its entire frontend analytics infrastructure:

- **Projected APY calculations** — weighted averages of yields across underlying protocols.
- **Benchmark comparisons** — transparent evaluation of performance against DeFi market rates.
- **Independent data validation** — APY figures sourced from vaults.fyi rather than self-reported.
- **Real-time tracking** — live performance data across DeFi and RWA strategies.

The integration was completed in days — enabling Nashpoint to launch with professional-grade analytics and credible, third-party-sourced data from day one.

## The Credibility Factor

Third-party verification creates compounding advantages:

**For investors:** Independent validation of performance claims removes a key source of doubt about Nashpoint's data integrity.

**For Nashpoint:** As Cormac explained, the transparency has an internal benefit too:

> _"It forces us to be good as managers too. Because if we're underperforming... we need to figure out if we're doing something bad, then we need to improve. By using a third-party institutional-grade analytics provider like vaults.fyi, there's no opportunity to 'play games' with displayed APY data."_

**For the industry:** This approach establishes a reproducible standard for transparency in onchain asset management — one where performance is validated by neutral, onchain-sourced data rather than protocol-reported figures.

## Results

- **Instant credibility** — industry-standard methodology and third-party validation from launch.
- **Development efficiency** — the team focused on core asset management, not data infrastructure.
- **Investor confidence** — independent validation supports institutional due diligence.
